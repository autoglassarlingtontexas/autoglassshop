Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our auto glass services

Do I need ADAS calibration after a windshield replacement? +
Many vehicles with windshield-mounted cameras require calibration after glass work. We check by VIN and options, then arrange static or dynamic calibration if needed.
How long before I can drive after replacement? +
Typical safe-drive time is about 60 to 90 minutes depending on adhesive, temperature, and humidity. Your technician will confirm the exact time.
Can you help with insurance claims? +
Yes. We help coordinate claims with major carriers to make the process simple and accurate.
What if it rains on my appointment day? +
We monitor weather and can perform covered mobile installs or reschedule. Your technician will advise the best option for a quality cure.
Which areas of Arlington do you cover? +
We service North, East, West, and South Arlington, the Entertainment District, Viridian, Pantego, Dalworthington Gardens, parts of Grand Prairie near Arlington, Mansfield (north), and Kennedale.
